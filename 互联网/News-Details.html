Seaside Investigate and Survey


Our group went to the Dameisha Beach Park to investigate people's need for our products.


We first investigated a man who swam in the sea but without any life saving devices. And he gave us some reasons for not wearing life saving devices: First, he thought he has mastered basic swimming skills, and he was not preparing to go to deep water area. Secondly, in his opinion, there were no adult men wearing life saving devices or swimming circles. And finally, the probability of drowning or sudden increase of sea waves is low (but it is typhoon day, the wave fluctuates obviously).
